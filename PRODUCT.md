# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Directive Films serves growth-minded service businesses, B2B companies, SaaS teams, and owner-led companies that need a stronger acquisition engine. The primary buyer is evaluating whether Directive Films can connect video, ads, AI, CRM, and sales into one system that creates customers instead of isolated marketing assets.

## Product Purpose

The site exists to make Directive Films feel like a premium growth partner. Success means a qualified buyer quickly understands the offer, trusts the team can own the whole journey from audit to closed deal, and books a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Premium, direct, tech-forward.

The voice should be confident and plainspoken. It should feel sophisticated and operational, not playful, fluffy, influencer-led, or overly agency-polished.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, template-like agency cards, soft creator-brand aesthetics, vague "full service" marketing language, and anything that feels decorative without proving business value.

## Design Principles

- Show the whole acquisition machine, not disconnected services.
- Lead with outcomes and operational clarity.
- Keep the tone premium and direct: fewer words, stronger claims.
- Use technology cues with restraint so the site feels capable, not gimmicky.
- Iterate new homepage ideas in `/sandbox/home` before promoting them to production pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, responsive layouts, readable line lengths, keyboard-accessible links and controls, and reduced-motion support for animated sections.
